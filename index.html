MANAGING YOUR STUDIES

Study Organization And Resources

Study Spaces

Even with effective time management, if the time allocated to studies is not used effectively, then then all the benefits of time management may not be seen. Appropriate places to study need to be established to ensure the time spent studying is effective.

A study space is an environment in which studies can be entirely focused on and can be done with little or no distractions. This may be a study area in the household, an empty room, or anywhere that the above conditions are met. While there are some that are most comfortably used like a bedroom, this can be distracting as the room is associated with sleep, or a place where leisure activities are conducted. This can lead distractions while studying and many do not realize this would not constitute as a suitable study space.

Organization

Time is not the only asset that needs to be organized. Making sure that the tools needed are always available will ensure that every situation is prepared for. Having a pen and paper or a laptop to take notes, having a ruler for graphs, or having the right program installed for the specific task is ensuring that the proper materials are available to effectively study. This applies to other aspects as well. Transport to intended destinations, or a plan for what material to study are prime examples of keeping organized.

Keeping on top of all tasks and getting all preparations organized beforehand will help in being prepared for any situation.
